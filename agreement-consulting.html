咨询顾问服务协议

Consulting Services Agreement

一、协议双方

甲方（服务提供方）：海南省索拉纳商务咨询中心

乙方（客户）：订单提交时填写的客户信息

二、服务范围

本协议适用于以下咨询顾问类服务：

  • 区块链咨询服务（基础咨询、年度顾问）
  • 美股开户咨询服务
  • 美股投资策略咨询服务
  • 美股上链咨询服务
  • 园区入驻咨询服务（基础、全套）
  • 政策申报服务
  • 投资咨询服务
  • 企业托管服务
  • 其他相关商业咨询服务

三、甲方权利与义务

3.1 甲方权利：

  • 有权要求乙方如实提供企业经营状况、财务数据等必要信息
  • 有权拒绝乙方提出的违法违规咨询要求
  • 有权按协议约定收取咨询服务费用
  • 有权根据实际情况调整咨询方案和建议

3.2 甲方义务：

  • 按照行业专业标准提供咨询服务
  • 基于乙方实际情况提供客观、中立的专业建议
  • 保护乙方的商业秘密和敏感信息
  • 及时向乙方反馈咨询进展和阶段性成果
  • 对咨询建议的合法性、合规性负责

四、乙方权利与义务

4.1 乙方权利：

  • 有权获得专业、客观的咨询建议
  • 有权了解咨询服务的进展情况
  • 有权对咨询方案提出意见和建议
  • 有权要求甲方对咨询建议进行解释和说明

4.2 乙方义务：

  • 如实提供企业经营状况、财务数据等必要信息
  • 按约定支付咨询服务费用
  • 配合甲方完成信息调研、现场考察等工作
  • 对甲方的专业建议给予必要的重视
  • 自行判断并承担采纳咨询建议的决策责任

五、咨询服务方式

5.1 基础咨询服务：

  • 线上咨询（微信、电话、视频会议）
  • 1-2次深度沟通（每次1-2小时）
  • 提供书面咨询报告
  • 后续问题解答（限定时间内）

5.2 年度顾问服务：

  • 定期沟通（每月至少1次）
  • 不限次数的临时咨询
  • 重大事项专项分析
  • 年度咨询报告
  • 行业资源对接

5.3 项目制咨询服务：

  • 根据具体项目制定服务方案
  • 可能包括现场调研、专家访谈等
  • 提供完整的项目咨询报告
  • 项目实施过程跟踪指导

六、服务费用与支付

6.1 费用构成：

  • 咨询服务费：甲方提供专业咨询的费用
  • 调研费用：如需现场调研、专家访谈等产生的交通、住宿等费用（如有）
  • 第三方费用：如需委托第三方机构进行专业评估等费用（如有）

6.2 支付方式：

  • 基础咨询（单次）：100%预付
  • 项目制咨询：50%定金 + 50%尾款（报告交付后）
  • 年度顾问：按季度或年度预付

七、交付成果

根据具体服务内容，甲方向乙方交付以下成果：

  • 咨询报告（书面或电子版）
  • 可行性分析报告
  • 投资建议书
  • 政策申报材料
  • 实施方案
  • 其他约定的咨询成果

⚠️ 重要说明：

咨询报告和建议仅供乙方决策参考，不构成投资建议或承诺。最终决策由乙方自行判断和承担责任。

八、服务期限

各项咨询服务的具体期限以订单确认时约定的时间为准：

  • 基础咨询：通常在1-2周内完成
  • 项目制咨询：根据项目复杂程度确定，通常1-3个月
  • 年度顾问：服务期限1年，可续约

如遇以下情况，服务期限可合理顺延：

  • 乙方未及时提供必要信息和资料
  • 政策调整导致咨询方案需要重新评估
  • 不可抗力因素

九、保密条款

9.1 保密范围：

双方应对以下信息承担保密义务：

  • 企业经营数据、财务数据
  • 商业计划、投资计划
  • 技术方案、业务模式
  • 客户信息、供应商信息
  • 咨询报告和建议
  • 其他标注为保密的信息

9.2 保密期限：

保密义务自知悉保密信息之日起生效，至该信息公开披露之日或双方另行约定之日止，但不得少于3年。

9.3 例外情况：

以下情况不受保密约束：

  • 信息已公开或已为公众所知
  • 依法律法规要求必须披露
  • 经对方书面同意披露

十、免责声明

以下情况造成的损失，甲方不承担责任：

  • 因不可抗力导致无法履行协议
  • 因政策调整、市场变化导致咨询建议失效
  • 因乙方提供虚假、不完整信息导致的咨询偏差
  • 因乙方未按建议执行或执行不当导致的损失
  • 因第三方（如政府部门、金融机构等）的原因导致的损失
  • 投资市场固有风险导致的投资损失

⚠️ 特别声明：

  • 区块链咨询：区块链技术和数字资产市场波动巨大，投资风险极高。甲方提供的咨询建议仅供参考，不构成投资建议。乙方应充分评估风险，自行决策。
  • 美股投资咨询：证券投资存在市场风险，投资需谨慎。甲方提供的投资策略和个股分析仅供参考，不构成买卖建议。最终投资决策和投资盈亏由乙方自行承担。
  • 政策申报：政策申报结果由政府部门审批决定，甲方不保证申报成功。甲方仅提供专业指导和材料准备服务。
  • 园区入驻：园区入驻条件由园区管委会确定，甲方不保证入驻成功。甲方仅提供咨询和对接服务。

十一、知识产权

11.1 咨询成果归属：

  • 甲方提供的咨询报告、分析方案等成果归乙方所有
  • 乙方有权在企业内部使用咨询成果
  • 未经甲方同意，乙方不得向第三方转让或公开发布咨询成果

11.2 方法论和工具：

  • 甲方使用的咨询方法论、分析工具等知识产权归甲方所有
  • 乙方不得将甲方的方法论和工具用于其他商业目的

十二、退款政策

12.1 可全额退款情形：

  • 咨询服务尚未开始，且乙方提出退款要求
  • 因甲方原因无法提供服务

12.2 部分退款情形：

  • 咨询服务已部分完成的，扣除已完成部分费用后退还余款
  • 年度顾问服务提前终止的，按实际服务时间结算，退还余款

12.3 不予退款情形：

  • 咨询服务已全部完成并交付成果
  • 因乙方原因（如提供虚假信息、拒绝配合等）导致无法继续服务
  • 因乙方主观判断与咨询建议不符导致的不满意（咨询本身无质量问题）

十三、违约责任

13.1 乙方违约：

  • 未按约定支付费用的，甲方有权暂停咨询服务
  • 提供虚假信息导致咨询偏差的，由乙方承担一切后果
  • 泄露甲方商业秘密的，应承担法律责任并赔偿损失

13.2 甲方违约：

  • 严重延期交付咨询成果的，应退还部分费用或提供补偿
  • 泄露乙方商业秘密的，应承担法律责任并赔偿损失
  • 提供的咨询建议存在明显违法违规内容的，应承担相应责任

十四、争议解决

双方在履行本协议过程中发生争议的，应首先通过友好协商解决。协商不成的，任何一方均可向甲方所在地人民法院提起诉讼。

十五、其他约定

  • 本协议自双方签署（乙方在线勾选同意）之日起生效
  • 本协议未尽事宜，双方可另行协商补充
  • 本协议的修改和补充协议与本协议具有同等法律效力
  • 甲方有权根据法律法规变化和业务调整对本协议进行修订
  • 咨询服务不构成法律建议、会计建议或投资建议，具体事项请咨询专业机构

I. Parties to the Agreement

Party A (Service Provider): Hainan Solana Business Consulting Center

Party B (Client): Customer information provided at order submission

II. Scope of Services

This agreement applies to the following consulting services:

  • Blockchain consulting services (Basic, Annual advisor)
  • US stock account opening consulting services
  • US stock investment strategy consulting services
  • US stock tokenization consulting services
  • Industrial park entry consulting services (Basic, Full service)
  • Policy application services
  • Investment consulting services
  • Corporate trusteeship services
  • Other related business consulting services

III. Rights and Obligations of Party A

3.1 Rights of Party A:

  • Right to request Party B provide truthful business operations, financial data, and other necessary information
  • Right to refuse illegal or irregular consulting requests from Party B
  • Right to collect consulting service fees as agreed
  • Right to adjust consulting plans and recommendations based on actual circumstances

3.2 Obligations of Party A:

  • Provide consulting services according to industry professional standards
  • Provide objective and neutral professional advice based on Party B's actual situation
  • Protect Party B's business secrets and sensitive information
  • Promptly update Party B on consulting progress and phased results
  • Responsible for legality and compliance of consulting recommendations

IV. Rights and Obligations of Party B

4.1 Rights of Party B:

  • Right to receive professional and objective consulting advice
  • Right to know consulting service progress
  • Right to provide opinions and suggestions on consulting plans
  • Right to request Party A explain and clarify consulting recommendations

4.2 Obligations of Party B:

  • Truthfully provide business operations, financial data, and other necessary information
  • Pay consulting service fees as agreed
  • Cooperate with Party A to complete information research, site visits, etc.
  • Give necessary attention to Party A's professional advice
  • Independently judge and bear decision-making responsibility for adopting consulting advice

V. Consulting Service Methods

5.1 Basic Consulting Services:

  • Online consulting (WeChat, phone, video conference)
  • 1-2 in-depth communications (1-2 hours each)
  • Provide written consulting report
  • Follow-up question answering (within limited time)

5.2 Annual Advisor Services:

  • Regular communication (at least once per month)
  • Unlimited ad-hoc consultations
  • Special analysis for major matters
  • Annual consulting report
  • Industry resource networking

5.3 Project-based Consulting Services:

  • Develop service plan according to specific project
  • May include on-site research, expert interviews, etc.
  • Provide complete project consulting report
  • Project implementation process tracking and guidance

VI. Service Fees and Payment

6.1 Fee Structure:

  • Consulting Service Fee: Fee for professional consulting provided by Party A
  • Research Fees: Travel, accommodation expenses for on-site research, expert interviews, etc. (if applicable)
  • Third-party Fees: Fees for professional assessment by third-party agencies (if applicable)

6.2 Payment Method:

  • Basic consulting (one-time): 100% prepayment
  • Project-based consulting: 50% deposit + 50% balance (after report delivery)
  • Annual advisor: Prepaid quarterly or annually

VII. Deliverables

According to specific service content, Party A delivers following results to Party B:

  • Consulting report (written or electronic)
  • Feasibility analysis report
  • Investment recommendation
  • Policy application materials
  • Implementation plan
  • Other agreed consulting results

⚠️ Important Note:

Consulting reports and recommendations are for Party B's decision-making reference only and do not constitute investment advice or commitments. Final decisions are made by Party B independently with responsibility.

VIII. Service Period

Specific period for each consulting service subject to time agreed upon order confirmation:

  • Basic consulting: Usually completed within 1-2 weeks
  • Project-based consulting: Determined by project complexity, usually 1-3 months
  • Annual advisor: Service period 1 year, renewable

Service period may be reasonably extended in following situations:

  • Party B fails to provide necessary information and materials in timely manner
  • Policy adjustments require consulting plan reassessment
  • Force majeure factors

IX. Confidentiality

9.1 Confidentiality Scope:

Both parties shall maintain confidentiality obligations for following information:

  • Business operation data, financial data
  • Business plans, investment plans
  • Technical solutions, business models
  • Customer information, supplier information
  • Consulting reports and recommendations
  • Other information marked as confidential

9.2 Confidentiality Period:

Confidentiality obligation takes effect from date of learning confidential information until information is publicly disclosed or otherwise agreed by both parties, but not less than 3 years.

9.3 Exceptions:

Following situations not subject to confidentiality constraints:

  • Information already public or known to public
  • Disclosure required by laws and regulations
  • Disclosure with written consent from other party

X. Disclaimer

Party A is not responsible for losses caused by following situations:

  • Force majeure preventing agreement performance
  • Consulting advice becoming invalid due to policy adjustments or market changes
  • Consulting deviations due to false or incomplete information provided by Party B
  • Losses due to Party B not following recommendations or improper execution
  • Losses due to third parties (government departments, financial institutions, etc.)
  • Investment losses due to inherent investment market risks

⚠️ Special Declarations:

  • Blockchain Consulting: Blockchain technology and digital asset markets are highly volatile with extremely high investment risks. Party A's consulting advice is for reference only and does not constitute investment advice. Party B should fully assess risks and make independent decisions.
  • US Stock Investment Consulting: Securities investment involves market risks. Investment requires caution. Party A's investment strategies and stock analysis are for reference only and do not constitute buy/sell recommendations. Final investment decisions and profit/loss are Party B's responsibility.
  • Policy Application: Policy application results are determined by government department approval. Party A does not guarantee application success. Party A only provides professional guidance and material preparation services.
  • Park Entry: Park entry conditions determined by park management committee. Party A does not guarantee successful entry. Party A only provides consulting and coordination services.

XI. Intellectual Property

11.1 Consulting Results Ownership:

  • Consulting reports, analysis plans, and other results provided by Party A belong to Party B
  • Party B has right to use consulting results internally
  • Without Party A's consent, Party B shall not transfer or publicly release consulting results to third parties

11.2 Methodology and Tools:

  • Consulting methodologies, analysis tools, etc. used by Party A's intellectual property belongs to Party A
  • Party B shall not use Party A's methodologies and tools for other commercial purposes

XII. Refund Policy

12.1 Full Refund Situations:

  • Consulting service has not started and Party B requests refund
  • Unable to provide service due to Party A's reasons

12.2 Partial Refund Situations:

  • Consulting service partially completed: refund balance after deducting completed portion
  • Annual advisor service terminated early: settled according to actual service time, refund balance

12.3 No Refund Situations:

  • Consulting service fully completed and results delivered
  • Unable to continue service due to Party B's reasons (false information, refusal to cooperate, etc.)
  • Dissatisfaction due to Party B's subjective judgment differing from consulting advice (consulting itself has no quality issues)

XIII. Breach of Contract

13.1 Party B Breach:

  • Failure to pay as agreed: Party A has right to suspend consulting services
  • Providing false information causing consulting deviation: Party B bears all consequences
  • Disclosure of Party A's business secrets: shall bear legal liability and compensate for losses

13.2 Party A Breach:

  • Serious delay in delivering consulting results: shall refund partial fees or provide compensation
  • Disclosure of Party B's business secrets: shall bear legal liability and compensate for losses
  • Consulting advice contains obvious illegal or irregular content: shall bear corresponding responsibility

XIV. Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising during performance of this agreement shall first be resolved through friendly negotiation. If negotiation fails, either party may file lawsuit with people's court where Party A is located.

XV. Other Provisions

  • This agreement takes effect from date both parties sign (Party B checks agreement online)
  • Matters not covered may be supplemented through separate negotiation
  • Amendments and supplements to this agreement have same legal effect
  • Party A has right to revise this agreement according to legal changes and business adjustments
  • Consulting services do not constitute legal advice, accounting advice, or investment advice. Please consult professional agencies for specific matters
