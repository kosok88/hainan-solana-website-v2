As the core platforms for the 2026 Customs Closure operations, these 13 hubs possess maximum autonomy and policy penetration. Select your operational base to unlock the highest **asymmetrical fiscal and tax advantages**.

Pressure Test Zone
#01

Yangpu Economic Dev Zone

The vanguard of the FTP. Focused on bonded processing, international trade, and maritime logistics. The primary operational ground for the "30% Value-Added Processing (VAP)" tariff exemption.

Core Dividend: Int'l Ship Registration, VAP Tax Exemption

Ideal For: Import/Export Mfg, Petrochemicals, Logistics Giants

Modern Services H.Q.
#02

Haikou Jiangdong New Area

The island's financial and corporate "Brain." The core carrier for multinational regional headquarters, offshore trade, and EFU (Free Trade Account) integration.

Core Dividend: QFLP/QDLP Funds, Aircraft/Vessel Leasing

Ideal For: Family Offices, Offshore Traders, MNC Regional HQs

Exclusive Medical Zone
#03

Boao Lecheng Medical Zone

China's sole medical special zone. Enjoys exclusive policies for licensed medical treatment, clinical research, and the global debut of innovative drugs and medical devices.

Core Dividend: Real-World Data (RWD), Zero-Tariff Medical Equipment

Ideal For: Int'l Pharma Giants, High-end Clinics, CROs

National Hi-Tech Zone
#04

Haikou National Hi-Tech Zone

Hainan's only national-level hi-tech zone. Focusing on biopharmaceuticals, advanced manufacturing equipment, and new materials. The top choice for physical economy entities.

Core Dividend: 15% CIT for Tech Firms, Factory Rent Subsidies

Ideal For: Biopharma Mfg, Precision Instruments, New Materials

Deep Sea & Seeds Peak
#05

Yazhou Bay Science & Tech City

The gathering place for national strategic scientific forces. Building an international intellectual property trading special zone for deep-sea technology and tropical agriculture (seeds).

Core Dividend: Top-tier R&D Subsidies, IP Offshore Securitization

Ideal For: AgTech R&D, Ocean Engineering, Elite Talent Teams

Consumption Core
#06

Sanya Central Business District

The commercial engine of the International Tourism Consumption Center. Focused on yachts, duty-free retail, art trading, and high-end private wealth management.

Core Dividend: "Zero-Tariff" Yachts, Bonded Art Displays, Consumer Finance

Ideal For: Yacht Industry, Luxury Retail HQs, Asset Managers

Commercial Aerospace
#07

Wenchang Aerospace City

Home to China's first commercial spacecraft launch site. Building the rocket, satellite, and data chains to create an international test zone for cross-border spatial data transmission.

Core Dividend: Launch Approval Facilitation, Aerospace Data Export Exemptions

Ideal For: Commercial Rockets, Satellite Comms, Spatial Big Data

Digital Economy Cluster
#08

Laocheng Tech City

(Upgraded from Hainan Eco-Software Park). A digital economy powerhouse with revenues exceeding 200 billion RMB. Focused on blockchain, digital entertainment, global gaming, and AI.

Core Dividend: Fast-track Game Licensing, Cross-border Data Security Channels

Ideal For: Global Gaming Studios, Web3/Blockchain, AI Algorithms

Customs Special Zone
#09

Haikou Comprehensive FTZ

The global distribution center for duty-free goods. Focused on cross-border e-commerce, duty-free warehousing, and high-value bonded maintenance and processing.

Core Dividend: "Inside Border, Outside Customs" Policy, E-commerce 1210 Model

Ideal For: Cross-border E-commerce, Duty-free Supply Chains

Global Edu Hub

Realizing "Study in Hainan = Study Abroad." Introducing top-tier foreign universities to run independently, pioneering international educational consumption loops.

Core Dividend: Independent Foreign Univ. Licensing, Zero-Tariff Edu Equipment

Ideal For: Top Global Universities, Global EdTech Enterprises

Digital Trade Gateway

Haikou Fuxing City Park

The gateway for influencer economy and digital trade. Gathering over 8,000 enterprises, focusing on cross-border data services, influencer livestreaming, and SaaS exports.

Ideal For: MCN Agencies, Cross-border Livestreaming, SaaS Providers

West Coast Mfg Base

A newly prioritized hub. Leveraging Basuo Deepwater Port, it focuses on high-tech materials, marine equipment manufacturing, and clean energy (offshore wind & hydrogen).
